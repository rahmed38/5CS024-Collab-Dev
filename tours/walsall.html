Welcome to Walsall Campus

Discover the charming Walsall Campus, known for its modern facilities and peaceful surroundings. Whether you’re here to study or relax, this campus offers everything you need for an inspiring educational experience.

Academic Buildings

The Walsall campus features a range of academic buildings designed for various fields of study. These buildings are equipped with the latest technologies and offer spacious classrooms and laboratories.

  • Building A - Arts and Humanities
  • Building B - Engineering and Design
  • Building C - Health and Social Care

Campus Facilities

Our campus provides top-notch facilities, ensuring that all students have access to everything they need for success. From well-equipped sports facilities to quiet study areas, you’ll find it all here at Walsall.

  • Sports Center with gym and courts
  • Library with 24/7 access
  • Coffee shops and student lounges

Green Spaces and Outdoor Areas

The Walsall Campus is surrounded by lush greenery, with many outdoor spaces where you can relax or have a break between classes. Enjoy peaceful walks through the campus gardens or study outdoors when the weather’s nice.
