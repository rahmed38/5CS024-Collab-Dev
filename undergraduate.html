Undergraduate Subjects

  • Bachelor of Business Administration

    This program provides a comprehensive understanding of business and management principles. It prepares you for leadership roles in various sectors.

    Career Paths: Business Manager, Marketing Executive, Entrepreneur

    Program Duration: 3-4 years

    Specializations: Marketing, Finance, International Business

  • Bachelor of Computer Science

    This program provides in-depth knowledge of computer systems, programming, and software development. You will learn to create innovative technology solutions for real-world problems.

    Career Paths: Software Developer, Web Developer, IT Specialist

    Program Duration: 3-4 years

    Core Modules: Programming, Data Structures, Algorithms, Networking

  • Bachelor of Engineering

    This program covers a wide range of engineering disciplines, providing a solid foundation in mathematics, physics, and engineering principles.

    Career Paths: Civil Engineer, Mechanical Engineer, Electrical Engineer

    Program Duration: 3-4 years

    Core Modules: Thermodynamics, Structural Analysis, Fluid Mechanics

  • Bachelor of Arts

    Gain a strong understanding of arts and humanities, and develop critical thinking, creativity, and communication skills.

    Career Paths: Journalist, Content Creator, Art Director

    Program Duration: 3 years

    Specializations: Literature, Philosophy, History, Sociology

  • Bachelor of Laws (LLB)

    This program provides the foundational knowledge required for a career in law. It will prepare you for further studies in law school or professional law certification.

    Career Paths: Lawyer, Legal Consultant, Corporate Lawyer

    Program Duration: 3 years

    Core Modules: Constitutional Law, Criminal Law, Contract Law

  • Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS)

    This medical program trains students to become doctors and healthcare professionals with comprehensive knowledge in medicine, surgery, and clinical practice.

    Career Paths: General Practitioner, Surgeon, Specialist Doctor

    Program Duration: 5-6 years

    Core Modules: Human Anatomy, Pathology, Pharmacology, Surgery

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the undergraduate program take?

The typical duration for an undergraduate program is 3 to 4 years, depending on the course and whether you choose full-time or part-time study.

Can I apply for a scholarship?

Yes, WLV offers a variety of scholarships for undergraduate students based on academic excellence, financial need, and other criteria. Check out our scholarships page for more information.

What are the entry requirements?

Entry requirements vary depending on the program. Generally, you'll need a high school diploma or equivalent, and sometimes specific subject qualifications or standardized test scores.
