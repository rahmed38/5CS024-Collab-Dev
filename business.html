Business

Gain a strong understanding of the business world, from management to marketing, finance, and international business practices. The Business program prepares students for a successful career in various business sectors.

  • Covers core business principles including marketing, finance, operations, and strategic management.

  • Focuses on global business operations, cross-cultural management, and international trade.

  • Studies consumer behavior, digital marketing, brand management, and marketing strategy.

  • Teaches financial reporting, taxation, audit principles, and business finance management.
