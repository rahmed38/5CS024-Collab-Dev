Visit Wolverhampton

Your guide to the best of Wolverhampton

City Highlights

Wolverhampton is a city rich in history and culture. The heart of the city features a mixture of modern architecture and historic landmarks. Key highlights include:

  • The Wolverhampton Art Gallery - A must-see for art lovers
  • The Grand Theatre - Home to a range of performances and musicals
  • The Mander Centre - A central shopping mall
  • Wolverhampton Wanderers FC - The local football team, based at Molineux Stadium
  • The Wolverhampton Clock Tower - A historical landmark

Transport Information

Wolverhampton is well-connected by multiple forms of transport:

Public Transport

The city has excellent bus and tram services that connect all major areas, including direct routes to the University of Wolverhampton. The city also has a central railway station, making travel to and from Wolverhampton easy and convenient.

Parking

Several car parks are located near the University, including:

  • Wolverhampton City Centre Parking - A large multi-storey car park with easy access to the main campus.
  • The University Car Park - Located close to the main buildings, for those visiting the University.

Walking Routes

The University of Wolverhampton is within walking distance from many key city landmarks. The main campus is easily accessible via pedestrian routes, and the surrounding areas have well-maintained walking paths and pedestrian zones.

Local Attractions

Explore the rich culture of Wolverhampton with these local attractions:

  • The Black Country Museum - A unique open-air museum showcasing the industrial heritage of the region.
  • West Park - A beautiful Victorian park offering plenty of space for relaxation and outdoor activities.
  • Wightwick Manor & Gardens - A beautiful National Trust property with exquisite gardens and a house full of pre-Raphaelite art.

Dining Options

Wolverhampton offers a variety of dining options, from traditional British fare to international cuisine:

  • The Royal London - A stylish pub offering hearty British dishes.
  • Villa Di Roma - A popular Italian restaurant located in the city center.
  • Havana - A vibrant Latin American restaurant offering tapas and cocktails.

Shopping Areas

Wolverhampton is home to several shopping areas, including:

  • The Mander Centre - A central shopping mall with popular retailers.
  • The Wulfrun Centre - A diverse shopping area with both high-street stores and independent boutiques.
  • Wolverhampton Market - A traditional market with fresh produce, goods, and local crafts.
