Arts

Dive into creativity through visual arts, graphic design, interior design, and photography. In Arts, you will explore various forms of creative expression and engage in both theory and practice in diverse artistic disciplines.

  • Develops practical artistic skills across various mediums (painting, sculpture, digital) while exploring contemporary art theory and creative practice.

  • Focuses on visual communication, digital design tools, typography, and creative problem-solving for print and digital media.

  • Combines architectural principles with spatial design, teaching technical drawing, 3D modeling, and sustainable design practices.

  • Covers both digital and traditional photography techniques, photo editing, studio lighting, and professional portfolio development.
