Social Sciences

Study the complexities of human society, behavior, and institutions. The Social Sciences programs offer an in-depth understanding of how individuals and societies interact, and provide opportunities to pursue careers in research, criminal justice, social work, and more.

  • Psychology BSc (Hons)

    Studies human behavior, mental processes, and research methods to understand the workings of the mind.

  • Criminology BA (Hons)

    Examines crime, criminal behavior, and the justice system, with an emphasis on the causes and consequences of criminal activity.

  • Sociology BA (Hons)

    Analyzes social structures, relationships, and contemporary social issues to understand societal dynamics.

  • Social Work BA (Hons)

    Prepares students for professional social work practice through theory and placement experience in various social service fields.
