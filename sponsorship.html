Our Sponsors & Partners

Supporting Excellence

Wolverhampton Wanderers Football Club

The University of Wolverhampton is proud to partner with Wolverhampton Wanderers Football Club, providing students with unique opportunities for career development, community engagement, and access to exclusive events at Molineux Stadium.

University of Wolverhampton Students' Union

The Students' Union is a vital partner of the university, offering a wide range of services, activities, and support to enhance student life. Through this sponsorship, the Students' Union helps create a vibrant, engaging environment for all students.

Local Food Vendors

Local food vendors around the campus provide students with affordable and diverse dining options. As part of the sponsorship, these vendors offer special discounts and promotions to enhance the student experience while supporting local businesses.

University-affiliated Charities

The University of Wolverhampton is committed to supporting charitable causes through partnerships with local and international charities. These sponsorships help raise awareness, encourage donations, and support important initiatives that benefit the community.
