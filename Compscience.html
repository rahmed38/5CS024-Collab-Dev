Science and Engineering

Delve into the fascinating world of technology, engineering, and scientific discovery. Our Science and Engineering programs equip you with the skills needed to solve real-world challenges in diverse fields such as computing, engineering, and mathematics.

  • Computer Science BSc (Hons)

    Covers programming, software development, and computer systems to equip students with the necessary technical skills to work in the tech industry.

  • Mechanical Engineering BEng (Hons)

    Studies mechanics, materials, design, and manufacturing processes to prepare students for careers in mechanical and civil engineering fields.

  • Construction Management BSc (Hons)

    Focuses on project management, building technology, and construction law, providing a strong foundation for leadership roles in the construction industry.

  • Mathematics BSc (Hons)

    Explores pure and applied mathematics, including statistical analysis and modeling, to provide key analytical skills applicable across industries.
