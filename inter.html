Welcome International Students!

Your journey to studying at the University of Wolverhampton starts here.

Why Choose the University of Wolverhampton?

The University of Wolverhampton is a welcoming and diverse place for students from all around the world. With a broad range of programs and support systems in place, we help our international students thrive academically and socially.

Support for International Students

We understand that studying abroad can be an exciting but challenging experience. Our international student support team is here to assist you with everything you need, from visa guidance to orientation, cultural events, and student welfare.

Applying as an International Student

The application process for international students may differ slightly from domestic applicants. We provide clear and easy-to-follow instructions to help you with your application, including information about required documents, deadlines, and English language requirements.

Campus Life for International Students

Our campus is home to a vibrant, multicultural student body. As an international student, you'll find numerous ways to get involved in student life, including cultural clubs, societies, and international student events that will help you settle in and build friendships.

Visa and Immigration Information

Our dedicated team can help guide you through the UK visa and immigration process, ensuring that you're fully prepared before you travel. We'll provide resources on student visas, financial requirements, and any other essential information you need for a smooth transition to life in the UK.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to speak English to apply?

Yes, a good level of English is required for most courses at the University of Wolverhampton. We offer English language support programs for students who need additional help.

What financial support is available for international students?

We offer scholarships and financial aid options for international students. Visit our Scholarships page for more details.

How can I contact the International Office?

You can reach the International Office by email, phone, or in person. Find all the contact information on our contact page.
