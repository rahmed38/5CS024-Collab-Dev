The Peer Mentorship Program at the University of Wolverhampton pairs new students with experienced mentors who provide guidance, support, and friendship throughout your academic journey. Our mentors are trained to help you adjust to university life and make the most of your experience here.

How the Program Works

1

Sign Up

Complete the registration form at the beginning of each semester to be matched with a mentor

2

Get Matched

We'll pair you with a mentor based on your course, interests, and personal preferences

3

Meet Regularly

Schedule in-person or virtual meetings with your mentor throughout the semester

4

Get Support

Receive ongoing guidance, resources, and advice from your mentor

Benefits of Joining

Personalised Guidance
