Supporting Your Mental and Physical Health

The University of Wolverhampton is committed to providing support and resources to help you maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle while studying. Whether you need help with mental health, physical wellbeing, or emotional support, there are various services and resources available to you.

Key Services Available:

  • 24/7 Mental Health Support
  • Campus Health Services
  • Counseling and Therapy Sessions
  • Fitness and Wellness Programs
  • Peer Support and Student Wellbeing Groups
  • Substance Misuse Support
  • Nutrition and Healthy Eating Resources
